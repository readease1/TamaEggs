Frequently Asked Questions

What is TamaEggs?
TamaEggs is a nostalgic digital pet game where you can hatch, care for, and evolve your own creatures while earning $TAMA tokens. It combines classic Tamagotchi gameplay with modern blockchain technology.
How do I earn $TAMA?
You earn $TAMA by playing mini-games, completing daily challenges, caring for your pet, and achieving milestones in the game. The more you play, the more you earn.
Is it free to play?
Yes, TamaEggs is completely free to play. Just load up the game and start hatching your egg. There are no hidden fees or pay-to-win mechanics.
What can I do with $TAMA tokens?
$TAMA tokens can be used for in-game purchases, special items, staking for passive income, and can be traded on supported exchanges. You have full ownership of your earned tokens.
How does my pet evolve?
Your TamaEgg evolves based on how you care for it. Feed it, play with it, and complete challenges to unlock new evolution stages. Different care patterns lead to different evolution paths.
Is there a mobile app?
Currently TamaEggs is playable through web browsers on any device. A dedicated mobile app is in development and will be released soon with additional features.
