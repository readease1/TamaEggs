> About

TamaEggs is an experimental on-chain project that combines nostalgic digital pet mechanics with autonomous crypto trading. Unlike traditional projects, there are no promises, no roadmaps guaranteeing success, and no human intervention in the creature's decisions.

This is a pure observation experiment. The creature lives, trades, and evolves (or dies) based entirely on market conditions and its own algorithmic behavior.

We are simply observers in this experiment. The outcome is uncertain, and that's the point.
