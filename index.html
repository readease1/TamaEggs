Frequently Asked Questions

What is TamaEggs?
TamaEggs is a fun, nostalgic digital pet game where you can hatch, care for, and evolve your own creatures while earning $TAMA tokens!
How do I earn $TAMA?
You earn $TAMA by playing mini-games, completing daily challenges, caring for your pet, and achieving milestones in the game!
Is it free to play?
Yes! TamaEggs is completely free to play. Just load up the game and start hatching your egg!
What can I do with $TAMA tokens?
$TAMA tokens can be used for in-game purchases, special items, and can be traded on supported exchanges!
How does my pet evolve?
Your TamaEgg evolves based on how you care for it! Feed it, play with it, and complete challenges to unlock new evolution stages!
